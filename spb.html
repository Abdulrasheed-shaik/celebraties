Additionally, he was honoured various state awards from Karnataka and Tamil Nadu governments,[14] four Filmfare Awards South and a Filmfare Award.[15] According to some sources, he held the Guinness World Record for recording the highest number of songs by a singer with over 50,000 songs in 16 languages.[18] On 8 February 1981, he set a record by recording 27 songs in Kannada in a single day, along with 19 songs in Tamil and 16 in Hindi, which has also been called a record.[13]
